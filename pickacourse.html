PickaCourse

Summer, 2020

Overview

I confirm my interest in frontend and project manager after this project. The project is completed by a group of 6 students. My role in the group is frontend developer and a communication bridge for designers and programmer.

Issue

Since Covid-19, everything went on online. Students find it hard to make friends on Zoom University. How can we build college-wise social media where students can find friends in the same school and classmates to share notes? What if there is a platform for students to preserve in-class/college connection into the future?

Survey Summary

  • The survey is distributed online open to Virgina Tech University students. 88 useful response in total.
  • 92.1%-94.4% Students agree or strongly agree that they want more student connections
  • 78.2% People had or currently have trouble making student connections
  • 83.9% People are interested in our solution of networking with fellow students and classmates

Product

A web-based college-wise social networking platform which allows students organizing around classes and schedules. After adding a course to your schedule, one will automatically join the class's group chat and follow other students in the same class. Connections are built based on similar schedule/ tracks/ interests. All you need is to pick a course!

Marketing Plan

Our target users: 19.9 million college Students in US (Prospective Students, Current Students, Alumni)

Our possible sponsorship: Companies that need new-grad employees, Ads, Colleges
