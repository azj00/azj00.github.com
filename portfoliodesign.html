My Main Personal Website

I built the website for my master application to Dartmouth Digital Arts program and Brown's Computer Science program. The admission teams are impressed by my portfolio and both offered me a seat in their programs. I designed the website to demonstrate my personal interests in both arts and computer science fields. To make it more engaging, I took the concepts of various cells represented my interests living in my brain. I drew all the characters and implemented the website with Bootstrap and JavaScript.
